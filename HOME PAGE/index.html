NIGHT CROWS

The game features 4 classes with 8 sub classes and 3 class advancement stages. The game intends to have huge PvP wars where three servers take part in a RvRvR battle with more than a thousand players, all of which will be able to battle for control of the land and air, as players will be able to take to the sky using gliders.

GODS UNCHAINED

Gods Unchained embodies the future of trading card gameplay. It taps into the rich world of marvelous mythical gods and their strengths and weaknesses, represented by over 1,500 unique cards.

DECENTRALAND

Decentraland is a three-dimensional online platform allowing users to be part of a shared digital experience where they can play games, socialize, and create, buy, or sell digital items.

ALIENWORLDS

The basic Alien Worlds gameplay has two primary layers. The first consists of mining on plots of land with your Shovel or other mining-related NFTs. The second layer consists of Syndicates, player-run, planetary governments that submit and vote on proposals for the Alien Worlds ecosystem using Trilium tokens allotted to their planet.
