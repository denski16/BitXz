SPLINTERLANDS, Inc.

It was founded by Matt Rosen and Jesse Reich in 2017. They are entrepreneurs and visionaries in the blockchain gaming space. Together, they have spearheaded the development and growth of Splinterlands, formerly known as Steem Monsters, which has garnered a dedicated player base and a strong presence in the NFT gaming community. Through their leadership, Splinterlands has evolved into a platform where players can collect, trade, and battle with digital cards, leveraging the power of blockchain technology and cryptocurrencies. Rosen and Reich's innovative approach has contributed to the success of Splinterlands as a leading player in the NFT gaming industry.
