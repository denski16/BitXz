SPLINTERLANDS

Splinterlands is an online collectible card game using blockchain technology. Every card in the game is owned as a non-fungible token (NFT) and is traded with bitcoin and ethereum.Splinterlands offers various game modes, including ranked battles, tournaments, and single-player campaigns, providing players with a diverse and engaging gaming experience. As players progress, they can earn rewards such as cryptocurrency tokens and rare cards, further enhancing the value of their collections.
