SPELLS OF GENESIS

Spells of Genesis (SoG) is the 1st blockchain-based mobile game ever made. SoG combines Trading Card Game functionalities with the point-and-shoot aspects of arcade games. Players have to collect and combine cards to create the strongest deck in order to fight their enemies. Collect,trade and combine orbs to build the strongest team and challenge various opponents while exploring the fantasy realm of Askian.
