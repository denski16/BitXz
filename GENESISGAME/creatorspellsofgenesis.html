EverdreamSoft

Shaban Shaame is an entrepreneur known for his contributions to the blockchain gaming industry. Under Shaame's leadership, EverdreamSoft developed Spells of Genesis, one of the pioneering blockchain-based trading card games. Released in 2015, Spells of Genesis was among the first games to utilize blockchain technology for in-game assets, allowing players to truly own their digital cards as non-fungible tokens (NFTs).
