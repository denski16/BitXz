PIXOWL

Sebastien Borget, one of the co-founders, is known for his expertise in blockchain technology and its applications in the gaming industry. He has been a vocal advocate for integrating blockchain and NFTs into gaming experiences to empower players with true ownership of in-game assets. Arthur Madrid, the other co-founder, brings extensive experience in mobile game development and business management to Pixowl. The company has released several successful titles and has become recognized for its creativity and innovation in the gaming space. Together, Borget and Madrid have led Pixowl in creating a diverse portfolio of games, including titles such as The Sandbox, Garfield: Survival of the Fattest, and Goosebumps HorrorTown. Their combined expertise and vision have contributed to Pixowl's success as a leading player in the mobile gaming industry.
